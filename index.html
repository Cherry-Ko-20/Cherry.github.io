About me

My name is Cherry Ko, a 24-year-old Data Scientist based in Seattle, WA. Armed with a 4-year degree in Business Administration, my professional journey began with a 6-month internship as a Business Analyst. Following that, a self-learning phase of 6 months as a Data Analyst fueled my passion for data. My learning journey also includes a dedicated 9-month work at the Triple Ten Data Science Bootcamp.

Proficient in SQL and Python, I navigate the data landscape with expertise in tools like Matplotlib, Excel, Tableau, and PowerBI. My journey extends into Machine Learning, covering both supervised and unsupervised learning techniques. At the core of my enthusiasm lies the joy of working with data, visualizing it, drawing meaningful conclusions, and making informed decisions.

Evident in over 10 practical projects during my Triple Ten experience, I invite you to explore my portfolio. Witness my learning journey and join me in unraveling the possibilities of data-driven insights. Your curiosity is encouraged, and I look forward to potential collaborations!
